01 — What's inside

Every knob you've ever wanted, turned all the way up.

Most word games hide their rules. Super Word hands them to you. Build the exact game you want to play — then play it against the toughest computer opponents on iOS, or anyone on Game Center.

01 / Board editor

Design your own boards.

15×15 classic, 17×17, 21×21, asymmetric layouts, custom premium squares. Draw it, save it, play it.

02 / Tile sets

Your letters, your values.

Swap in custom distributions, point values, and blanks. Bring any language or ruleset you want to play.

03 / Dictionaries

Bring your own words.

ENABLE ships bundled. Import any plain-text word list — SOWPODS, TWL, house rules, all fair game.

04 / Computer opponents

Five AI tiers, your level.

From a gentle Novice that helps you learn up to a Grandmaster that will push your game. Pick your match.

05 / Move review

See the move you missed.

After every turn, compare your play against the AI's best move on the same rack. A quiet way to get better.

06 / Multiplayer

Challenge humans over Game Center.

Turn-based matches against friends and strangers. Pick up where you left off, on any of your devices.

07 / Principles

Free. And free of nonsense.

No ads. No coins. No timers. No accounts. Just the game, forever. Built by a player who got tired of the others.

08 / Sync

iPhone and iPad, same game.

Games, boards, tile sets, and dictionaries follow you across devices. Start on the train, finish on the couch.

09 / Native

Fast, offline, private.

Built as a proper iOS app. Plays instantly offline. No tracking, no analytics, no background chatter.

02 — In the app

A board, a rack, and very little else.

The interface stays out of your way. Every pixel earns its place. Drag‑to‑place tiles, shuffle with a swipe, commit with a tap.
